European Social Forum Of Cyprus

E.S.F.C

The European social Forum of Cyprus (ESFC) is a result of 21 years of hard social work by a team of young people from Active Citizens that took part in many non-governmental organizations (NGOs) experienced in the Social course of the Cypriot population in all levels. The ESFC as an institution consists of a Pancyprian Network of NGOs with non-profit targets, which cover various interests on all the possible subjects that preoccupy each organized group of Cypriots.

ESFC AIMS:

  • The Materialization of programs Nationwide.
  • The promotion of ideas regarding networks from NGOs in Cyprus.
  • he benefit of modern services for the members of NGOs providing the suitable technical infrastructure and know-how.
  • The proper usage of financing resources worldwide.
  • The training of NGO executives in the modern requirements of the Society of Citizens.
  • Training of Teachers (trainers)

ESFC cooperates with the following organizations.

  • Pan Cyprian Organization for rehabilitation of invalid persons.
  • The friends of the disabled in Limassol.
  • The association of parents and friends of children with special needs.
  • The parents association of the children’s infirmary of the Red Cross.
  • The parents association of St Lucas special school.
  • The “ St John” center for senior citizens.
  • The association of persons with eye problems.
  • The Pan Cyprian association for thalassemia patients.

President of ESFC: Chrisis Michailidis
