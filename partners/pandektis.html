Our Partners

PANDEKTIS

Rehabilitation Center

Pandektis Rehabilitation center belongs to ESFC family and it is located in Limassol, Cyprus. Pandektis is a specially designed center for people with special needs and people with disabilities. It was establish in 2008 with main mission to offer a holistic and quality approach to rehabilitation .

The Rehabilitation center offers the following treatments:

  • Kinesiology and kinesiotherapy
  • Physiotherapy
  • Manual therapy and massage
  • Kinisiforo K-SET Rehabilitation
  • Postoperative and posttraumatic rehabilitation
  • Speech therapy
  • Psychological support
  • Dietician
  • Occupational therapy
  • Technician for prosthesis
  • Home nursing
  • Special Adapted exercise Programs
  • Transportation with 2 special adapted cars for wheelchairs
