KINISIFORO

a Smart Rehabilitation System

Major advantages of Kinisiforo

  • Simultaneously exercise arms, legs and torso muscles
  • Operator can elect and adjust precisely weight bearing and force acting on lower extremities during the exercise
  • Elliptical motion can be adjusted independently for one and another leg according to patient characteristics
  • Zero vibration
  • Operator can choose from passive mode, active mode and combined passive-active mode. Passive mode can be changed to active mode and vice versa quickly by decision of the patient
  • The machine follows the patients motion and helps only where it is necessary, and not the patient follows passively the machine motion like in other devices
  • Operator can adjust precisely intensity and speed of the exercise
  • It is the only machine that can produce symmetrical balance and elliptical motion during one pedal stride without any acceleration
  • Patient doesn't require extra assistance from therapist or others during exercise, but the therapist has an easy access for assistance or evaluation during the exercise where it is needed
