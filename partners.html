European Social Forum Of Cyprus

E.S.F.C

The European social Forum of Cyprus (ESFC) is a result of 21 years of hard social work by a team of young people from Active Citizens that took part in many non-governmental organizations (NGOs) experienced in the Social course of the Cypriot population in all levels. The ESFC as an institution consists of a Pancyprian Network of NGOs with non-profit targets, which cover various interests on all the possible subjects that preoccupy each organized group of Cypriots.

PANDEKTIS

Rehabilitation Center

Pandektis Rehabilitation center belongs to ESFC family and it is located in Limassol, Cyprus. Pandektis is a specially designed center for people with special needs and people with disabilities. It was establish in 2008 with main mission to offer a holistic and quality approach to rehabilitation.

Orthocyprus (K.O.E)

Orthocyprus (K.O.E) is an orthotic and prosthetic center established in 2006 in Limassol. Main activity of the center is to help people with orthopedic problems and disabilities, offering them the most suitable products and professional services that meet their needs.
