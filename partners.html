Our Partners

PANDEKTIS

Rehabilitation Center

Pandektis Rehabilitation center belongs to ESFC family and it is located in Limassol, Cyprus. Pandektis is a specially designed center for people with special needs and people with disabilities. It was establish in 2008 with main mission to offer a holistic and quality approach to rehabilitation .

The Rehabilitation center offers the following treatments:

  • Kinesiology and kinesiotherapy
  • Physiotherapy
  • Manual therapy and massage
  • Kinisiforo K-SET Rehabilitation
  • Postoperative and posttraumatic rehabilitation
  • Speech therapy
  • Psychological support
  • Dietician
  • Occupational therapy
  • Technician for prosthesis
  • Home nursing
  • Special Adapted exercise Programs
  • Transportation with 2 special adapted cars for wheelchairs

Manager of Pandektis: Hadjionisiforou Onisiforos

Orthocyprus (K.O.E)

Orthocyprus (K.O.E) is an orthotic and prosthetic center established in 2006 in Limassol. Main activity of the center is to help people with orthopedic problems and disabilities, offering them the most suitable products and professional services that meet their needs.

The center has established a very good cooperation over the years with the most professional and well known brands in Europe like Otto-Bock, Ossur, RSL Steeper, Be-Bionic, Fior & Gents, AFT International, North Sea Plastics and Prolution.

Orthocyprus has a well-equipped workshop and expert personnel for any prosthetic/orthotic project, from the manufacturing to the successful rehabilitation. Using the latest technology and best quality material, Orthocyprus team is able to manufacture from the smallest prosthetic or orthotic braces (lower or upper limb), to the most complicated with electronic control, myoelektric or bionics.

The product range expands to many other well known brands, and offers, over the shelf braces and supports, rehabilitation products, mobility aids and medical equipment.

Vision of Orthocyprus for the future:

  • To help people in rehabilitation and full restoration.
  • To create permanent and lasting relationships with our patients.
  • To constantly improve the quality and standards of our services.
  • And finally: TO KEEP UP OUR PROMISES!

European Social Forum Of Cyprus

The European social Forum of Cyprus (ESFC) is a result of 21 years of hard social work by a team of young people from Active Citizens that took part in many non-governmental organizations (NGOs) experienced in the Social course of the Cypriot population in all levels. The ESFC as an institution consists of a Pancyprian Network of NGOs with non-profit targets, which cover various interests on all the possible subjects that preoccupy each organized group of Cypriots.

ESFC AIMS:

  • The Materialization of programs Nationwide.
  • The promotion of ideas regarding networks from NGOs in Cyprus.
  • he benefit of modern services for the members of NGOs providing the suitable technical infrastructure and know-how.
  • The proper usage of financing resources worldwide.
  • The training of NGO executives in the modern requirements of the Society of Citizens.
  • Training of Teachers (trainers)

ESFC cooperates with the following organizations.

  • Pan Cyprian Organization for rehabilitation of invalid persons.
  • The friends of the disabled in Limassol.

  • The parents association of St Lucas special school.
  • The "St John" center for senior citizens.
  • The association of persons with eye problems.
  • The Pan Cyprian association for thalassemia patients.

President of ESFC: Chrisis Michailidis
