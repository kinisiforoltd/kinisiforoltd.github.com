About Company


Kinisiforo Ltd was founded in 2011, and has a main office in Limassol, Cyprus. Company specializes on equipment for rehabilitation in neurologic conditions and is run by people, who clearly understand the need of a patient.

Our main product is a cutting-edge rehabilitation system Kinisiforo. This system was designed as a result of long-term clinical and scientific research and implements a new method of neurological rehabilitation K-Set. This method was developed in collaboration with PANDEKTIS Rehabilitation Center (Limassol, Cyprus), that has a significant experience in rehabilitation for all neurologic conditions, including cerebral palsy, stroke, tetraplegia, spinal cord injury and many others.

Kinisiforo Ltd constantly works on improvement for its products and maintains strong competitive capabilities with the best quality and the highest efficiency. We have the latest health ideas, better health product and perfect services to serve every customer on an individual basis.

If you are interested in Kinisiforo Rehabilitation System or new method for neurologic rehabilitation K-Set, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are looking forward to establishing successful business relationships with clients around the world.
